    WELCOME TO VANAVIHARI RESORTS

    Vana Vihari promotes sustainable and community-based eco-tourism, fostering local empowerment and environmental conservation. It aims to enhance living standards for indigenous communities while providing nature enthusiasts with an immersive and environmentally conscious experience.

    VANAVIHARI

    Vana Vihari is a community-based eco-tourism resort in Maredumilli — a sleepy hamlet in the East Godavari district of Andhra Pradesh. You can properly unplug from the outside world and step into a jungle at Vana Vihari Resort.

    JUNGLE STAR ECO CAMP

    Discover the magic of nature at Jungle Star Eco Camp, where every leaf tells a story and every sound is a melody of the wilderness.

    VANYAVIHARI

    Nestled in nature's embrace, VANYAVIHARI Resort invites you to unwind, rejuvenate, and lose yourself in the luxury of the wild.

    RAMPA WATERFALLS

    Rampa Waterfalls, where the cascade of nature's beauty mesmerizes every soul – a symphony of water and wilderness.

    CORINGA WILDLIFE SANCTUARY

    Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary, where the symphony of nature orchestrates a breathtaking harmony of flora and fauna.
